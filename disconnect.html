How to Disconnect This App from QuickBooks Online

This internal script is connected to your QuickBooks Online account via OAuth 2.0. You can revoke its access at any time directly from within your QuickBooks Online account.

Steps to Disconnect:

  1. Log in to your QuickBooks Online account.
  2. From the left-hand navigation menu, click on "Apps".
  3. Navigate to the "My Apps" tab.
  4. Find the application named KH Reno in the list of connected apps.
  5. Click on the action menu (often represented by three dots or an arrow) next to the app name.
  6. Select "Disconnect" from the menu.
  7. Follow the prompts to confirm the disconnection.

Once disconnected, the script will no longer be able to access your QuickBooks Online data until it is re-authorized.
